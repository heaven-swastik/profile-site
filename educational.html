Educational Background

I completed my secondary and higher secondary education at Uttarpara Children's Own Home (UCOH) under WBBSE and WBCHSE, respectively. Currently, I'm pursuing B.Tech at Institute of Engineering and Management (IEM), Newtown, affiliated with University of Engineering and Management Kolkata (UEMK).

- Secondary Education (10th): Uttarpara Children's Own Home (UCOH), West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE)
- Higher Secondary Education (12th): Uttarpara Children's Own Home (UCOH), West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE)
- Entrance Exams: JEE Mains and WBJEE
- Undergraduate Studies: Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) at Institute of Engineering and Management (IEM), Newtown, affiliated with University of Engineering and Management Kolkata (UEMK).
